At Grin Living, we don't just manufacture bedsheets — we build market-ready textile solutions engineered for commercial success.

With a fully integrated, state-of-the-art facility in Surat, Gujarat, we manage every step of the manufacturing journey in-house — from yarn sourcing and high-definition digital printing to precision stitching and retail-ready packaging.

Our philosophy is straightforward: provide our B2B partners with consistent product quality, predictable supply timelines, and market-tested designs that yield high inventory turnover and superior profit margins.
